# WMv2 Phase 3 — Forensic Traces

*Every number in a posterior-conditioned forecast, traced from a verified as-of claim through the five planes
to the terminal. Generated by `experiments/wmv2_phase3_forensic_dump.py` (real DeepSeek + live Google News
RSS); machine-readable in `experiments/results/phase3/forensic_traces.json`. Retrieval date is recorded in the
artifact (live news drifts, so exact numbers reproduce only against the frozen bundle hash).*

The purpose of these traces is anti-scaffolding: to show the posterior CROSSING every plane
(EVIDENCE→POSTERIOR→WORLD-STATE→EXECUTION) and being CONSUMED, with each terminal-affecting number
attributable to specific claims and a fixed observation model — never to an LLM-minted probability.

---

## Trace 1 — "Will the Federal Reserve cut interest rates at its next meeting?" (as-of 2024-09-01)

**EVIDENCE plane.** 17 documents retrieved live; **13 included claims** (span-verified, as-of); bundle hash
`ab7fbfa2fb4fde1b`.

**EVIDENCE→POSTERIOR: claims → qualitative tags → dependence collapse.** The LLM tagged each claim
qualitatively (direction/strength/is_strategic — NO numbers); reliability was set from the source-type table
(0.75 = news). The 13 claims collapsed by Phase-2 `dependence_group` into **8 effective observations**:

| effective observation (dep group) | direction | copies collapsed |
|---|---|---|
| dep_44f321892 | neutral | 1 |
| dep_15ce6994a | neutral | 1 |
| dep_298050c8c | supports_no (strong) | **2** |
| dep_4326ffe92 | supports_no (moderate) | 1 |
| dep_aa7968e31 | supports_yes (strong) | 1 |
| dep_7f202a51c | neutral | **3** |
| dep_7d41f5fdb | supports_no (from strong+moderate+neutral) | **3** |
| dep_524f34a8d | neutral | 1 |

Six of the 13 raw claims were syndicated/related copies (three groups of 2–3) — counted ONCE each. This is the
dependence correction the ablation showed is load-bearing: without it these copies would triple-count.

**POSTERIOR plane.** Prior = generic weakly-informative `Beta(1,1)` (mean 0.5, `transport_risk: high` — no
reference-class data was wired, honestly labeled, NOT called a reference class). Each effective observation
reweighted the 400 rate particles through `DirectionalRateModel.likelihood` (sens/spec from the fixed strength
table, flattened by reliability). Assimilation ledger (ESS per step): 400 → 365.6 → 326.4 → 365.2 → … → 305.2,
no resample triggered (ESS stayed healthy). Net: **posterior mean 0.344 (±0.250), shift −0.156** — the balance
of evidence (three no-groups vs one yes-group + four neutral) pulled the rate below the prior. Structural
posterior: `data_dependent` 0.60→0.68, `forward_guidance` 0.30→0.32, `political_pressure` 0.10→**0.0003**
(evidence essentially ruled out the political-pressure structure).

**WORLD-STATE plane.** `plan.posterior_rate_particles` = the 400 posterior particles; `plan.structural_
posterior` = {data_dependent 0.68, forward_guidance 0.32, political_pressure 0.0003}. Latent specs
materialized (both measurable — evidence-linked AND consumed):
- `outcome_rate` → `bounded_continuous`, consumed by `['generic_outcome_prior:rate', 'resolve_outcome:lean']`
- `structural_hypothesis` → `discrete_structural`, consumed by `['materialize:_run_with_hypotheses:stratum_weight']`

**EXECUTION plane.** The resolver drew each particle's Bernoulli rate from the posterior particles; the
terminal `StateDelta.uncertainty.rate_source == "posterior"` (read back = proof of consumption). Strata
allocated by the structural posterior (`structural_source == "phase3_evidence_posterior"`). **Terminal:**
`{no_cut: 0.77, cut: 0.23}`, support grade `highly_speculative` (broad prior + tier-6 mechanism), forecast
present (no abstention).

Every number above came from a claim + a fixed table + a likelihood update. The LLM supplied only the
qualitative direction/strength of each claim.

## Trace 2 — "Will there be a US federal government shutdown?" (as-of 2024-09-15)

**EVIDENCE plane.** 15 included claims → **8 effective observations** after dependence collapse.

**POSTERIOR plane.** Prior `Beta(1,1)`. **Outcome-rate posterior mean 0.535 (±0.276), shift +0.035** — the
directional evidence was mixed, so the RATE barely moved. The action was in the **structural posterior**:

| structure | prior | posterior |
|---|---|---|
| hardline_block | 0.40 | **0.9823** |
| bipartisan_compromise | 0.40 | 0.0153 |
| short_term_patch | 0.20 | 0.0024 |

The `StructuralDetectionModel` log-likelihoods concentrated almost all structural mass on the "hardline block"
structure — the evidence identified WHICH causal world holds, even though it left the base rate uncertain.
This is the representation principle in action: the discrete structural latent carried information the
continuous rate could not.

**WORLD-STATE→EXECUTION.** Particle strata allocated 98% to the hardline-block structure, whose lean drives
the terminal toward a shutdown; `rate_source == "posterior"`, consumed. **Terminal:** `{shutdown: 0.64,
no_shutdown: 0.36}`, grade `highly_speculative`, forecast present.

## What these traces demonstrate (Part T)

1. **No number is LLM-minted.** Directions/strengths are qualitative LLM tags; every probability, reliability,
   likelihood, and weight comes from a fixed registered table or a prior×likelihood update.
2. **Dependence correction is real and visible.** 13→8 and 15→8 collapses, with `n_collapsed` per group in the
   ledger; syndicated copies count once.
3. **The posterior is CONSUMED, not stored.** The terminal `StateDelta` records `rate_source == "posterior"`
   and `structural_source == "phase3_evidence_posterior"`, read back from the actual execution.
4. **Rate and structure carry different information.** Trace 1 moved the rate (−0.156) with a mild structural
   update; Trace 2 barely moved the rate but sharply identified the structure (0.40→0.98) — justifying keeping
   both representations distinct.
5. **Uncertainty is honest.** Both keep a wide posterior SD (±0.25–0.28) and a `highly_speculative` grade —
   weak-but-real evidence widens rather than fabricates confidence, and never blocks the forecast.
